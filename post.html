Catering
Our culinary team presents enticing buffets as well as tempting à la carte options throughout the day. Market fresh selections are prepared to order at seven different live cooking stations, which draw influence from Western, Indian, and Asian cuisines. The martabak station showcases a local favorite—stuffed pan-fried bread—while premium beef and Japanese ingredients are masterfully combined at the specialty noodle counter. Additionally, SuperFoodsRx™ powerhouse selections such as cinnamon-spiced oatmeal and whole-grain pikelets are always a nutritious choice.
